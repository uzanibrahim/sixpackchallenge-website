# Privacy Policy

**6Pack Crunch Challenge** respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personal data on our servers.

The app allows users to:
- Take or select photos for before and after comparison
- View and manage their fitness progress locally on their device

All photos and related data are stored **only on the user’s device**.

## Photo Access

The app may request access to your device camera or photo library solely for the purpose of:
- Taking before and after photos
- Comparing fitness progress

Photos are **not uploaded**, **not shared with us**, and **not accessible to other users**.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

If you choose to share your photos using your device’s system share options (such as WhatsApp or other apps), this action is entirely controlled by you and occurs **outside of the app**.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services that collect personal data.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is not specifically directed at children.  
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are in control of your information.  
We do not maintain servers that store user data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.  
Any changes will be reflected on this page.
