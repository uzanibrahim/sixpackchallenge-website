Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

6Pack Crunch Challenge ("we," "our," or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process personal data on our servers.

The app allows users to:

  • Take or select photos for before and after comparison
  • View and manage their fitness progress locally on their device
  • Track workout completion and streaks

All photos, progress data, and related information are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

Photo Access

The app may request access to your device's camera or photo library solely for the purpose of:

  • Taking before and after progress photos
  • Selecting existing photos for progress comparison
  • Viewing your fitness transformation over time
Important: Your photos are never uploaded to any server, shared with us, or accessible to other users. All photo data remains on your device under your complete control.

Data Storage

All app data, including:

  • Progress photos
  • Workout history
  • Personal settings and preferences

...is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers that store user data. This means you have complete ownership and control over your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

If you choose to share your photos or progress using your device's native share functionality (such as sharing to social media, messaging apps, or email), this action is entirely controlled by you and occurs outside of our app's scope.

Third-Party Services

The app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain device information for the purpose of serving relevant ads. This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Apart from advertising services, the app does not integrate third-party analytics or tracking services that collect personal data.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for general audiences and is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Using a device passcode or biometric lock
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Regularly backing up your device

Your Rights

Because all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can:

  • Delete your progress photos at any time through the app
  • Clear all app data through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all associated data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
